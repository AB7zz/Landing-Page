About Us


HILL Educational Consultancy is a high powered, passion driven company with more than 40 years combined homeschooling and educational experience. HILL offers complete solutions for parents who are just beginning their homeschooling journey. In addition, providing support and assistance to our more experienced homeschoolers.


Vision

To become the premium brand in providing high quality, personal & specialized homeschooling services, while maintaining trusted and strategic relationships in the global education market.

Mission

As an emerging home-education consulting service, we aim to offer bespoke solutions, developed with you in mind: Improving the lives of brave parents and their children, who have embarked upon a home education journey Facilitating and easing the process of homeschooling, offering expert advice and a proven track-record of success Empowering parents to choose the best education that fits their individual needs Guiding home educators on how to strengthen their skills and practices


About the Founder



Michaela Cooper is a veteran American Educator, holding nearly 30 years of teaching experience in both the US and UAE. Her Early Childhood Education background led her to teach both Lower and Higher Primary in a British Curriculum private school, whilst also serving as an English HoD here in the UAE. She is one of the Co-Founders of DUNEHA (Dubai Northern Emirates Homeschool Association), the first UAE based homeschooling group - providing support, guidance and social opportunities to its members. She is the only remaining Founder residing in the UAE. She excels in Homeschooling Curriculum Design and Instruction, whilst also enjoying time as a passionate Writer and Editor. A mentor to prospective homeschoolers and educators globally, Michaela is also an expert homeschooler, having homeschooled her own 5 children over a period of 28 years. She is continuously supporting both homeschooled and classroom learners, as well as their parents.
